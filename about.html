who we are

Cam Energy is a Brooklyn based, family owned heating, ventilation, plumbing and air conditioning company specializing in boiler service and repair. Anthony Kenny started the company with a vision of using his experience to help people in New York City, The Boroughs, Long Island & Westchester with anything related to boilers, plumbing and HVAC, as well as give employees a chance to learn from himself & provides a great place to work. There isn’t a job too big or too small, we’ll take a look at every and any boiler, burner, oil tank and plumbing job.

Cam Energy currently holds an oil-burner installer’s license for the State of New York.

We look forward to working with you in the future.
